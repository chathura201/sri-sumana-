About Us

Sri Sumana Maha Pirivena

Sri Sumana Pirivena is a renowned Buddhist educational institution located in Mudduwa, Ratnapura, Sri Lanka. Established with the noble purpose of preserving and propagating the teachings of the Buddha, our institution has been a beacon of spiritual learning for generations.

We offer comprehensive Buddhist education including Pali language, Dhamma studies, meditation practices, and monastic training. Our dedicated faculty of experienced monks guide students on their spiritual journey, nurturing both wisdom and compassion.
